# Pricing and Plans

## Summary
Alchemy plans are primarily based on compute units and product access. Choose a plan that matches your expected traffic and data needs.

## Guidance
- Estimate CU usage from your request profile.
- Use staging keys to validate expected CU consumption.
- Monitor usage in the dashboard and adjust.
